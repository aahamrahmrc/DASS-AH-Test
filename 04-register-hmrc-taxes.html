Register for HMRC taxes

What to do next

To continue with this registration you must have a Government Gateway account. If you don't have a Government Gateway account one will be created for you as part of the registration process.

Please note: You can't register two different businesses for the same business tax with HMRC using a single Government Gateway account. For example, to register two businesses for VAT you must have a separate Government Gateway account for each business.

You can use this service if you're a company that has been dormant and wants to tell HMRC that you're now within the charge to Corporation Tax.

Once your registration application has been processed HMRC will automatically enrol each business for the online service associated with the taxes you've registered for. You'll then be able to file your returns online.

You'll have only 90 days to complete the registration from the date you start to fill it in. The information you enter will be saved automatically as you move through the pages.

To register the business, please click the 'Next' button below.
